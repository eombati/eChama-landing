4. Platform Description

eChama provides software tools for cooperative savings groups ("chamas") to:

  • Track member contributions and group balances
  • Manage group membership, roles, and invitations
  • Create and approve case events (benefit claims)
  • Process member contribution payments via ACH bank transfer through Stripe
  • Link a group bank account via Plaid to sync transactions and match contributions
  • Generate reports and maintain an audit trail of all financial activity

6.1 Contribution payments

Contribution payments processed through eChama represent voluntary transfers from a member to a group's savings account. Because these funds are deposited directly into your group's bank account and are not held by eChama, refunds of contribution payments are at the sole discretion of your group administrator. To request a refund of a contribution, contact your group administrator directly.

6.2 Disputed transactions

If you believe a payment was processed in error or without your authorisation, you must notify us within 30 days of the transaction date by contacting support at support@echama.com or calling 651-317-9215. We will investigate and work with Stripe to resolve the dispute. We aim to respond to dispute enquiries within 3 business days.

6.3 Platform subscription fees

Platform subscription fees are non-refundable after 30 days from the billing date. If you believe you were charged in error, contact us within 30 days and we will review your request.

6.4 Auto-Pay deductions

If you are enrolled in Auto-Pay, an ACH debit will be initiated automatically when your group administrator approves a case event. You will receive an email notification prior to and following the deduction. To dispute an Auto-Pay charge, contact us within 30 days. To prevent future Auto-Pay deductions, unenroll from Auto-Pay in your Payments settings before the case event is approved.

How to raise a dispute: Email support@echama.com with the subject line "Payment Dispute — [Your Name] — [Transaction Date]", or call 651-317-9215. Include your name, group name, amount, and date of the transaction.
